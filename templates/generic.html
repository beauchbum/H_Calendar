Frequently Asked Questions!

Things keep breaking. Are you sure this is the finished product?
Apologies in advance for any headaches. I built this alone...in my spare time...and most of it went like this. Please feel free to reach out with any issues at guppysound@gmail.com

I tune into someone, but then I don't hear any music?
There may not be a playable device available. Try opening up Spotify on your device and then play some music. It should sync up as soon as your account goes online.

Why do I keep hearing Desafinado by Stan Getz and Charlie Byrd...?
When you pause Spotify for a few seconds, your device goes offline and cannot be accessed remotely. So to prevent this issue, Desafinado plays whenever the person you are listening to is inactive.

Why doesn't it work without Spotify Premium?
Guppy relies on Spotify metadata to play music. Some of this vital data is only available to Premium users.
